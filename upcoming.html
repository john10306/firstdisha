Watering Device

I love green and I love plants. I would like to take care of my plants every day, but hey, I am a future engineer and it's summer. So either I am at school, work, or running around with friends.  To make life easier I am planning on to make a device that would water the plant automatically.
Now, developing a design shouldn't be a problem. I can create a solid model of the design and 3D print it, unless i want to make it super creative. Well, for now I want to do something simple, such as figuring out how the sensors will work and understand the circuitry. Once the hardware part is taken care of, design will be on it's way.
 I decided to play with Arduino a bit. Well Today it's all about blinking.....​
