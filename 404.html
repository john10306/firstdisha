About Me
​
Hi! I am Disha, an undergraduate student in the City College of New York. I'm currently a full-time student majoring in Mechanical Engineering. My current career goal is to gain experience for my field from hands-on work as well as from a classroom setting. It’s my passion to improve life for everyone through mechanical engineering. I enjoy developing engineering designs that make many projects more efficient and safer for the environment. I love manufacturing a design and seeing my developed creation come to life. There are always improvements to be made and I aspire to be better at what I do. Through active learning and an enthusiasm to help others, I plan to innovate myself.
